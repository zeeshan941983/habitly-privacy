🔒 Habitly is built with privacy first. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Introduction

EisZee Apps ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Habitly mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data when you use our App on Android devices.

By using Habitly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. Habitly is a fully offline app. All data you create — habits, streaks, completion history, reminders, notes, and achievements — is stored locally on your device only using on-device storage (Hive database).

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to EisZee Apps or any third party.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

No name or email No location No device ID No analytics No tracking No account required No ads No third-party SDKs
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not collect location data of any kind
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IMEI, or advertising IDs
  • We do not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking tools
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not require you to create an account or log in

4. Local Notifications

Habitly uses local notifications to remind you to complete your habits. These include simple reminders, alarm-style reminders, and interval-based reminders (e.g. water every 2 hours).

All notifications are scheduled entirely on your device using Android's notification system. No notification content is transmitted to any server. EisZee Apps has no visibility into when or how your notifications fire.

5. App Permissions

Habitly requests the following Android permissions. None of these are used to collect or transmit personal data:

POST_NOTIFICATIONS
To send habit reminder notifications on Android 13 and above
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM
To schedule reminders at precise times so they fire at exactly the time you set
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
To automatically reschedule your reminders after your device restarts
VIBRATE / WAKE_LOCK
To vibrate your device and wake the screen for alarm-style reminders
USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT
To show alarm reminders on the lock screen when your device is sleeping
ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY
To allow critical alarm reminders to override Do Not Disturb mode

6. Data Storage and Security

All Habitly data is stored locally on your device using Hive, a lightweight encrypted database. EisZee Apps has absolutely no access to this data at any time.

If you uninstall Habitly, all your data is permanently and automatically deleted from your device. We have no backup of your data.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with third parties because we collect no user data. There is nothing to share.

8. Children's Privacy

Habitly is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any users, there is no specific risk to children using the App.

9. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Access — All your data is visible inside the app at all times
  • Delete — Delete any habit or all data from within the app settings, or by uninstalling the app
  • Export — Use the Backup feature in Settings to export your data as a JSON file
